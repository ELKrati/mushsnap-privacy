📋 Information We Collect

MushSnap is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide the service.

  • Photos: Images you submit for mushroom identification. These are sent to our AI provider for processing and are not stored on our servers permanently.
  • Scan history: Identification results stored locally on your device via AsyncStorage. We do not have access to this data.
  • Purchase information: Managed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store your payment details.
  • Anonymous usage data: Basic app usage metrics via RevenueCat to manage subscriptions.
We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

⚙️ How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Provide mushroom identification results via AI analysis
  • Manage your subscription status and scan limits
  • Improve the accuracy of identification over time
  • Respond to support requests if you contact us

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

🔗 Third-Party Services

MushSnap uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI (GPT-4o mini): Images are sent to OpenAI's API for visual analysis. OpenAI's privacy policy applies to this processing. Images are not used to train their models by default.
  • RevenueCat: Handles subscription management and in-app purchase verification. Subject to RevenueCat's privacy policy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: Handle all payment processing. We never see your payment details.

🔒 Data Storage & Security

Your scan history is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. Photos submitted for identification are processed in real-time and not stored permanently.

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect any data we do process. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

👶 Children's Privacy

MushSnap is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

⚖️ Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any data processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

⚠️ Safety Disclaimer

MushSnap is an AI-powered tool for educational purposes only. Never consume any wild mushroom based solely on this app's identification. Always confirm with a qualified mycologist or expert before consuming any mushroom found in the wild. Misidentification can be fatal.

We are not liable for any harm resulting from the use or misuse of mushroom identification information provided by this app.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
